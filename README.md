# 🪔 Diwali Sales Analysis (Python Project)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on Diwali sales data to uncover customer behavior, purchasing patterns, and key business insights. The goal is to identify high-value customer segments and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Python  
- Pandas  
- NumPy  
- Matplotlib  
- Seaborn  

---

## 📁 Files Included
- `Diwali_Sales_Analysis_project1.ipynb`  
- `Diwali Sales Data.csv`  

---
## 📈 Project Workflow
- Data Cleaning and Preprocessing  
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)  
- Data Visualization using Matplotlib & Seaborn  
- Business Insights Generation  

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- Majority of buyers are **female**, with higher purchasing power than males  
- The **26–35 age group** contributes the highest to overall sales  
- Top performing states: **Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, Karnataka**  
- **Married women** are the most active and high-spending customers  
- Customers from **IT, Healthcare, and Aviation sectors** contribute significantly  
- Most purchased categories: **Food, Clothing, Electronics**  

---


## 🎯 Conclusion
Married women aged **26–35 years**, from **Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka**, working in **IT, Healthcare, and Aviation sectors**, represent the most valuable customer segment. They are more likely to purchase products from **Food, Clothing, and Electronics categories**, making them a key target group for business growth and marketing strategies.
